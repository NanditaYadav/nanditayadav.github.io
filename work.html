Work Experience

Product Manager Intern

During my internship at Yapply.LLC, I had the opportunity to work as a product manager and collaborate with product managers and the development team to effectively fulfill project objectives. Utilizing Agile methodology, I was able to prepare the PRD and build lo-fi and first prototype mockups for the first version of the platform. In addition to this, I also conducted daily scrums, mapped out weekly sprint planning, and monitored project timelines to ensure timely delivery of the final product.
Working as a product manager intern was a valuable learning experience for me. It allowed me to hone my skills in project management and product development, while also giving me the opportunity to work alongside experienced professionals in the industry. The experience of working on a real-world project gave me a glimpse into the challenges and intricacies of managing a product, from ideation to final launch.
Overall, my experience as a product manager intern at Yapply.LLC has been instrumental in shaping my understanding of the software development industry, and I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked with such an experienced and talented team.

Operations Analyst Intern

Working as an operations analyst at Home.LLC was a challenging yet rewarding experience for me. My role was to manage all operational activities of the Customer Success Team as needed. I was responsible for designing and managing various social media adverts that helped in increasing the customer base and building the culture policies for the organization.
One of the key tasks that I enjoyed the most was curating and preparing data from proprietary data sources. It involved a lot of research and analysis, but the results were always worth the effort. I also worked on maintaining details on CRM software that helped in streamlining the customer service process.
Working on various projects gave me the opportunity to learn new skills and enhance my existing ones. I had the chance to work on a diverse range of tasks, from managing day-to-day operations to contributing to long-term projects that helped the company grow. It was a great experience to work with a team of dedicated professionals who were always willing to help and support each other.
Overall, my experience as an operations analyst at Home.LLC was an enriching one, and it has helped me grow both personally and professionally.

Graphic Designer

Participating in a hackathon is a thrilling experience, and being part of the organizing team takes it to a whole new level. I had the opportunity to be part of the graphic designing team for IIIT Naya Raipur’s first hackathon, and it was an experience that I will never forget.
My role was to design banners and posts for marketing and outreach purposes. To do this, I used Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. It was exciting to see my designs come to life and be shared on various social media platforms. I designed over 10 banners and posts that helped attract more participants and make the event a success.
The hackathon saw registrations from over 700 participants, spanning 150+ colleges across 9 countries. Being a part of the team that made this event a reality was a rewarding experience. I gained valuable skills in graphic designing and teamwork that I will carry with me throughout my career.
Overall, being a member of the graphic designing team for IIIT Naya Raipur’s first hackathon was a challenging but rewarding experience. It taught me the importance of teamwork, communication, and creativity in organizing and promoting successful events.
